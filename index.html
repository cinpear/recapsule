So... what is this?

Every year, tons of companies come out with their version of a "wrapped", recapping some part of your year. Let's make our own.
  1. Spend at least 5 hours making a wrapped. Track your time in hackatime.
  2. Ship it! and provide a video demo.
  3. Submit your project by Jan 14th and get a $25 grant to buy a blind box.

FAQs

Am I eligible?

You're eligible if you're between 13 and 18 years old (inclusive).

What's a wrapped?

Search for Spotify Wrapped, Discord Checkpoint, or YouTube Music Recap for examples!

Can I work in a team?

No, please work individually.
